Why I Love Traveling

Traveling helps me experience new cultures, see beautiful landscapes, and step out of my comfort zone. I love the feeling of exploring somewhere new—every place has its own personality, food, and scenery.

My Favorite Places I’ve Visited

  • Hawaii – beautiful beaches and sunsets
  • New York City – energy, food, and Broadway
  • California – beaches + road trips
  • Utah National Parks – hikes and views

My Travel Bucket List

  1. Greece – White villages and crystal waters
  2. Italy – Hidden towns and authentic Italian cuisine
  3. Japan – Ancient temples and cherry blossoms
    • Tokyo – city life and ramen
    • Kyoto – temples and traditional tea houses
    • Hokkaido – winter snow festival
  4. Switzerland – Mountains and lakes
  5. Amsterdam – Tulip fields and biking culture
